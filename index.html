INTERMEDIATE SPEAKING TASK

Topic: Healthy Lifestyle

Instructions: Prepare and deliver a 2–3 minute oral presentation about a healthy lifestyle. You may use notes, but do not read your speech word for word.

Speaking Task

Imagine that your school is organizing a Health Awareness Week. You have been asked to give a short talk to your classmates about the importance of maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

In your presentation, you should:

  1. Explain what a healthy lifestyle means.
  2. Describe at least three healthy habits teenagers should practice.
  3. Explain the benefits of these habits.
  4. Mention one challenge teenagers face.
  5. Suggest one practical solution.

Guiding Questions

  • What activities can help people stay healthy?
  • Why is regular exercise important?
  • How does a balanced diet affect our health?
  • What problems prevent teenagers from living healthily?
  • What advice would you give to your classmates?
